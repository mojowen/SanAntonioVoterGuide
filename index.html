Ballot Measures

Proposition 1

What it does

Edwards Aquifer Protection Renewal

A yes Vote Means...

San Antonio can continue to protect the aquifer and land above it, securing our primary water supply

endorse

A no Vote Means...

The program to protect and improve the Edwards Aquifer will lose its funding.

endorse

Recommended: yes

Charter Amendment 2

What it does

City Council Pay

A yes Vote Means...

Effective July 1st, City Councilors will be paid $45,722 per year and the Mayor will be paid $61,725.

endorse

A no Vote Means...

City Councilor and Mayoral pay will stay the same: Councilors will earn $1,040 per year and the Mayor will earn $4,040 per year.

endorse

Recommended: yes

Proposition 2

What it does

Linear Trails Program

A yes Vote Means...

San Antonio can expand its system of trails and parks around the city

endorse

A no Vote Means...

San Antonio's parks will lose a major source of funding.

endorse

Recommended: yes

Charter Amendment 1

What it does

Public Vote for Railway Projects

A yes Vote Means...

A public vote will be mandatory for any railway project that the City wants to push forward.

endorse

A no Vote Means...

City Council can decide to approve or fail railway projects in San Antonio

endorse

Recommended: yes
